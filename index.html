Hi! I'm Jared Lebel

I'm a multi disciplinary graphic designer with a love for branding from Ottawa Canada. I believe that traditional design principles combined with innovation, exploration and badassery can create impactful ideas and design solutions. Mostly, I like to make things work well and look good.

Iron North — A beacon of northern light, inspiring it’s community.

+ + + + + +

Iron North

logo + identity + assets

Collaboration at Salt

When ownership changes in an organization, whether a partnership ends or a new owner, sometimes evolution is necessary. Iron North was looking to evolve their brand so it reflected the new ownership’s values more closely while retaining their community spirit. A star atop a mountain of type, coupled with medieval inspired house symbolism, Iron North’s new identity and assets reflect strength, community, and fun.

Study No 4/6

Algonquin College — Redesigning an identity for an organization at the edge of evolution.

+ + + + + +

Algonquin College

identity standards + identity application + illustration + art direction

Collaboration at Salt

We were absolutely honoured when Algonquin College approached Salt in our second year to take a stab at their Strategic plan document which flowed into a long term working relationship where we revamped all of their identity standards. Retaining only the logo and colour palette, we re designed the applicastion of their branding and helped to roll out most of the application.

A designer, musician, oddball and notorious over-thinker, I graduated from design school and began a journey with a partner to build a branding studio In Ottawa known as Salt. We did that, and we did it pretty well, working with a multitude of organizations form small startuos to lareg organizations, such as Algonquin College. It's been a wild ride and I'm ready to see where the journey takes me next.

Eager to see what’s on the other side of this newly opened door, I’m looking to continue my passion for graphic design and transform the noise in my mind into clear and empathetic brands and designs, whether it’s to join a team, help a startup, elevate a large organization, or anything in between.
